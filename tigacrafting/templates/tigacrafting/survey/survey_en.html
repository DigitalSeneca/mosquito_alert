Want to help us?

We’re always researching ways to improve and better reach citizen scientists interested in studying and monitoring mosquitoes. We’ve developed this short survey and we’d be glad to hear from you. It will take less than 5 minutes and your feedback will help advance scientific knowledge of citizen participation in ecological monitoring projects.

Your answers will remain anonymous and will provide insight into our citizen scientists.

Take the survey in english.

The survey is part of a study developed by Mosquito Alert, CEAB-CSIC, and UPF.
